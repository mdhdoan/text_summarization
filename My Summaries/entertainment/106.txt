The Streets, aka British rapper and songwriter, Mike Skinner have topped a poll to find the year's best album.Streets have 'album of the year'Rock band U2's latest album How to Dismantle an Atomic Bomb came fourth while Razorlight's Up All Night was in fifth place.A Grand Don't Come for Free beat Keane's Hope and Fears in second place with Franz Ferdinand's self-titled album third, in the Q magazine poll.Others notables on the list included Dizzee Rascal in at number 12 and The Zutons at 20.
