The five writers in line for the award won their respective categories - first novel, novel, biography, poetry and children's book - on 6 January.

A panel of judges including Sir Trevor McDonald, actor Hugh Grant and writer Joanne Harris will decide the overall winner on Tuesday.

Geraldine McCaughrean is the final contender, having won the children's fiction category for the third time for Not the End of the World.

Novelist Andrea Levy is favourite to win the main Whitbread Prize book of the year award, after winning novel of the year with her book Small Island.

As well as writing poetry, Symmons Roberts also makes documentary films.
