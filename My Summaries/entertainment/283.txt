Essex act Prodigy are to headline the second stage at this year's V Festival, joining main stage headliners Scissor Sisters and Franz Ferdinand.

Other bands at the sold-out festival include Queens of the Stone Age, The Killers, Keane, The Streets and Foo Fighters.

It will be the Chemical Brothers' only UK festival performance of the year.

Tickets for the V festival go on sale on Friday 11 March.

The Manchester band will play on 9 July at Scotland's biggest festival.
