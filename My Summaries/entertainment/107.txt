The Manic Street Preachers are to take over the helm of BBC digital radio station 6 Music for a week in December.

The Welsh band, whose hits include Design For Life and If You Tolerate This, have already chosen which records to play between 6 and 12 December.

Manics in charge of BBC 6 Music

The band will also answer questions e-mailed in by 6 Music listeners.

Their albums of the day include Who's Next by The Who and U2's Joshua Tree.
