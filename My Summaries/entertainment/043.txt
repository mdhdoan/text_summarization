Hollywood stars including Spike Lee, Burt Reynolds and Oscar nominee Alan Alda have paid tribute to actor Ossie Davis at a funeral in New York.

Friends and family, including actress Ruby Dee his wife of 56 years, gathered at the Riverside Church on Saturday.

"Ossie was my hero, and he still is," said Aviator star Alan Alda, a family friend for over forty years.

Also present at the service was former US president Bill Clinton and singer Harry Belafonte, who gave the eulogy.
