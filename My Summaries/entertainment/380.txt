Clint Eastwood's Million Dollar Baby re-entered the charts at number eight, following its recent success at the Oscars.

The film, which scored Oscar nominations for Don Cheadle and British actress Sophie Okonedo, made £507, 596 in its first week of nationwide release.

The boxing drama, which won four awards including best film and best director, has made £4.4m to date.

Hide And Seek, which was knocked off the top of the US box office by Boogeyman last month, fell one place.

The film, in which a young man is forced to revisit a traumatic childhood experience, took £788,439 in its first three days on release.
