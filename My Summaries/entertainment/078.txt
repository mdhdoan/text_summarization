Clint Eastwood's boxing drama Million Dollar Baby has become the new favourite to win best picture at the Oscars on Sunday.

In the supporting categories, Cate Blanchett is tipped to win for her performance as Katharine Hepburn in The Aviator, while Morgan Freeman is favourite to take home his first ever Oscar, for Million Dollar Baby.

Million Dollar Baby has seven nominations while The Aviator has 11.

Anne Thompson of the Hollywood Reporter said that Million Dollar Baby was "heart-wrenching".
