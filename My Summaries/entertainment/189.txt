Michael Buerk's return to Ethiopia, 20 years after originally reporting its famine, topped the factual category for BBC programme This World.

Holmes' 800m gold medal victory beat favourite moments from drama, comedy and factual programmes, as voted by television viewers.

The 2004 TV Moments will be shown on BBC One at 2000 GMT on Wednesday.

Natasha Kaplinsky's Strictly Come Dancing win was top entertainment moment and a Little Britain breast feeding sketch won the comedy prize.

She then went on to take the overall prize of Golden TV Moment.
