At Leeds, Iron Maiden will headline the first day of the festival on the Friday, Pixies will follow on Saturday and the Foo Fighters will close the event on Sunday.

Pete Doherty's band Babyshamblers will appear on the NME/Radio One stage along with Kasabian, Black Rebel Motorcycle Club and Bloc Party.

It will the first time the veteran British metal band have played Reading in 23 years.

The Pixies will headline the first day of Reading, while Foo Fighters will play on Sunday and Iron Maiden will close the event.

They are the Pixies' and Iron Maiden's only UK festival gigs, while Foo Fighters are also at T In The Park.
