US actor Jamie Foxx has been given two nominations for Golden Globe awards, with Meryl Streep, Morgan Freeman and Cate Blanchett also up for prizes.

The Golden Globes ceremony will take place on 16 January, with the Oscars following on 27 February.

The Golden Globes, Hollywood's second most prominent awards, are the first major nominations to be announced.

The stars were shortlisted on Monday for supporting roles, with the main nominations still to come.

Sean Penn, Charlize Theron, Tim Robbins and Renee Zellweger all won acting awards - mirroring the eventual Oscars outcome.
