Shadow foreign secretary Michael Ancram told MPs a new White Paper was part of trying to soften up opinion ahead of the referendum on the EU constitution.

The Tory spokesman also criticised the government for claiming the EU constitution would make Europe easier to understand.

Security, stability and prosperity would be the key themes when the UK took over the chairmanship of the EU in July, said Mr Straw.
