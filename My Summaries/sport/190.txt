Top seeds South Africa return to the scene of one of their most embarrassing failures when they face the Seychelles in the Cosafa Cup next month.

Mauritius host the first group, and their opponents are Madagascar, the Seychelles and South Africa.

Seychelles v South Africa

The teams are now divided into three group of four and play knock-out matches on successive days to determine the group champions.

Coach Stuart Baxter and his squad will return to Curepipe face the Seychelles in their first game of the new-look regional competition.
