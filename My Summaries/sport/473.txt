The stats speak for themselves: 11 titles from 11 finals during 2004 - three of them Grand Slams - and 13 final victories in a row going back to Vienna 2003.

The number one player in the world is also the main man for promoting the sport off court.

Now he just needs to find a way of winning the French Open, the one Grand Slam to so far elude him.

According to all those players and even Federer himself, the Swiss star is in a different league.

Hewitt, at times in Houston, showed form which easily matched his Grand Slam-winning efforts of 2001 and 2002.
