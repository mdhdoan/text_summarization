Steve Finnan believes the Republic of Ireland can qualify directly for the World Cup finals.

Switzerland, Ireland, France and Israel are all now tied on five points from three matches - although the Republic look to have a slight edge after claiming away draws in Basel and Paris.

After Saturday's superb display in the draw in Paris, Ireland face minnows the Faroe Islands in Dublin on Wednesday.

"There is a chance for us now to go on, win our home games and why not win the group, even though it's a tough one," said the Liverpool player.
