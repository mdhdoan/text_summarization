Carlos Moya described Spain's Davis Cup victory as the highlight of his career after he beat Andy Roddick to end the USA's challenge in Seville.

Aged 18 years and 185 days, the Mallorcan became the youngest player to win the Davis Cup.

Spain's only other Davis Cup title came two years ago in Valencia, when they beat Australia.
