Koubek, who defeated Britain's Greg Rusedski in the decisive rubber of the Davis Cup in September, is now set to miss the start of the season.

He said, "A three-month ban would mean that I not only will miss the Australian Open, but also the Davis Cup in Australia."

The world number 60 failed a routine drugs test at this year's French Open but now plans to lodge an appeal.
