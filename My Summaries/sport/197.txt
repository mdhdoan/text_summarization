Mateja Kezman, the scorer of Chelsea's goal, needed treatment on a head injury during the match after being hit by a missile, believed to be a coin.

A spokeswoman for Chelsea said the club would await the referee's report before deciding on its course of action.

The FA is to take action after trouble marred Wednesday's Carling Cup tie between Chelsea and West Ham.

The FA is already looking into the trouble at Tuesday's heated Carling Cup tie between Millwall and Liverpool.

The FA will seek reports from the clubs and the police, and will review video evidence and the referee's report.
