It is also the toughest game you are likely to play, as hordes of enemies and few lives pile the pressure on.

Simply choose your grunt and wade through five 2D side-scrolling levels of the most hectic video game blasting you will ever encounter.

But at a time when blockbuster titles offer only a fresh lick of paint in favour of real innovation, Metal Slug 3 is a fresh gasp of air from an era when the Xbox was not even a twinkle in Bill Gates' eye.

Like some drill sergeant from the past, Metal Slug 3 is a wake-up call to today's gamers molly-coddled with slick visuals and fancy trimmings.

But a more frantic shooter you will not find at the end of your joypad this year.
