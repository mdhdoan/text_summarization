3G technology lets people take, watch and send video clips on their phones, as well as swap data much faster than with 2G networks like GSM.

People with 3G phones in the UK can already download football and music clips on their handsets.

3 is looking to music as a way of persuading more people to take up the latest video phones.

Rock outfit Rooster are playing what has been billed as the first ever concert broadcast by phone on Tuesday evening from a London venue.

The company behind the trial hopes to learn more about how people use their video phones.
